Mission

My mission could be divided in 3 points



Travel This is the point I love more, the point I believe in more. Travelling gave me lots of opportunities,
I appreciated the importance of being alone, the importance of meeting new people, taking the best and the worst from them.
I put all my efforts to pursuit this goal and I think that the Developer job allows this kind of lifestyle and it has a good impact on nature.
Please share with me my best moments, you'll find them on my social pages linked at the end of this page.

Personal Growth I can honestly say that I'm obsessed by learning new stuff. I love the idea that we can put things in our brain everyday.
Maybe being curious is what keeps us alive, it help us not to be bored in life or maybe it is what helps me.
Main topics I'm interested in are: languages, lifestyle, biographies, travel experiencies and new tech .

Create Last but least is creating. This point is what my soul reveals, how I can express my creativity, how I visualize things.
Creating to me means I can explore inside me and show it to the world outside, not only in my job.
Every brick is important in our personal building and I actually think we're limited on our own so we need new connections as well.
Have a look at the Portfolio section to see what I mean.
